https://blog.lqdev.cn/2018/11/12/springboot/chapter-thirty-four/

1. 配置 Apache CXF

   https://cxf.apache.org/

   在 pom.xml 中加入以下代码：

   ```
           <dependency>
               <groupId>org.apache.cxf</groupId>
               <artifactId>cxf-spring-boot-starter-jaxws</artifactId>
               <version>3.4.0</version>
           </dependency>
   ```
